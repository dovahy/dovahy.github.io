Introduction

Hunter x Hunter is a manga series written and illustrated by Yoshihiro Togashi. The series received an animated adaptation in 1999, which follows the story until the end of the Greed Island Arc. Later in 2011 the series received another adaptation, which follows till the end of the Election Arc. Hunter x Hunter is one of the most famous shounen series. The manga is currently on hold (Hiatus) with chapter 400 being the newest chapter currently. Due to Togashi's health issues, the series is often in hiatus, meaning, it is not released in the weekly shounen jump. The author now changed to a monthly shounen program, to adjust the release to his health issues. I created this webpage to show my love to this amazing series. I will undergo a summary throughout the arcs of the series - later I will also introduce the main characters and give my own thoughts to the themes and characters, that Togashi created.

Hunter x Hunter genre are: Fantasy, Adventure, Drama, Martial Arts
